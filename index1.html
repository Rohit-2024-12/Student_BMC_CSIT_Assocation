Semester wise CSIT Notes

Complete semester wise notes for CSIT.

Introduction to Information Technology
0 Subjects
First Semester
5 Subjects
First semester in B.Sc. CSIT helps to create the fundamental foundation of technology, electronic circuits, programming, and arithmetic to urge you started with the journey of B.Sc. CSIT.
Second Semester
5 Subjects
The second semester of B.Sc. CSIT focuses on elementary ideas of object-oriented programming approach, problem-solving, and advanced ideas of electronic circuits and boards.
Third Semester
5 Subjects
The third semester of B.Sc. CSIT focuses on the foundation of information structures, mathematical modeling, the design of computers, ideas of graphics, and applied mathematics measures together with hypotheses and varied tests.
Fourth Semester
5 Subjects
Fourth semester of B.Sc. CSIT introduces state machines and their languages, the conception of the pc networks and operating systems, the inspiration of the database management system and a glimpse of underlying principles of artificial intelligence.
Fifth Semester
6 Subjects
Fifth semester of B.Sc. CSIT is an introduction to basic components of design and analysis of pc algorithms, software development cycle, underlying principles and design of cryptosystems, modeling and simulation of various kinds of systems, elementary ideas of web development, multimedia, and image processing.
Sixth Semester
6 Subjects
Sixth Semester of B.Sc. CSIT focuses on developing ideas of software package cycles and testing, style of the compiler, e-governance, .NET framework, technical writing, hardware design, e-commerce, automation, and artificial intelligence.
Seventh Semester
5 Subjects
B. Sc. CSIT of Seventh Semester focuses on advance java programming. This semester also focus on Data warehouse, Data Mining and Network Security.
Eight Semester
4 Subjects
The Eight Semester of B. Sc. CSIT focus on advance database management systems and Networking. In this Semester, Student can implement their knowledge to solve real world problems.
